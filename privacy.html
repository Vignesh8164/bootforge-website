LEGAL & PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026 • Version 2.4

01. Local-First Data Philosophy

BootForge is engineered with a strict Local-First and Zero-Telemetry policy. All operating system ISO parsing, dual-partition table creation (FAT32 + exFAT), file streaming, and raw SCSI block sector writing occur 100% locally on your Android device. Your ISO images, personal files, and drive contents never leave your device.

02. Android Permissions & Hardware Access

BootForge requests specific Android system permissions strictly necessary for low-level USB Host operations:

  • USB Host Permission (`android.permission.USB_PERMISSION`) — Used exclusively to claim the connected OTG flash drive interface via userspace SCSI (`libusb`). BootForge does NOT access private app storage, photos, or contacts.
  • Storage Read Access — Used solely to allow you to select your desired operating system ISO file (e.g. Windows 11, Ubuntu) from your phone's storage.

03. Payments & Transaction Security

BootForge Pro license transactions ($2.00 USD) are processed directly and securely through PayPal via 256-bit SSL encrypted connections. BootForge does not collect, handle, or store credit card numbers, bank details, or PayPal passwords. PayPal provides transaction verification to activate your lifetime APK license.

04. Zero Third-Party Analytics or Spying

BootForge contains no third-party advertising SDKs, no behavioral trackers, and no background analytics engines. Kernel logs and write speed metrics displayed in the app are strictly runtime telemetry kept in RAM during active sessions.
